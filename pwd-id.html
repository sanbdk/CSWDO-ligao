Required Documents for Assistance

Persons with Disability (PWD) ID and Booklet Issuance

  • Duly accomplished PWD Application Form (available at CSWDO)
  • Disability Certificate or Medical Assessment from a licensed physician or government-accredited hospital/clinic
  • 2 pcs. 1x1 ID pictures
  • Barangay Certificate of Residency
  • Birth Certificate (or any valid proof of identity and age)
  • If for replacement: Affidavit of Loss (if ID or booklet is lost)

Application Process

  1. Secure the PWD Application Form from the City Social Welfare and Development Office by downloading it at our website.
  2. Fill out the form completely and prepare all the required documents listed above.
  3. Submit the documents to the CSWDO officer-of-the-day for initial review.
  4. Proceed to the designated program focal for screening, document validation, and interview if needed.
  5. Wait for the processing of the PWD ID and booklet. The processing period usually takes 3â€“5 working days.
  6. Claim the ID and booklet from the CSWDO once available. You will be asked to sign a release logbook upon claiming.
  7. Use the ID and booklet to access benefits, discounts, and services provided by law to qualified PWD individuals.
