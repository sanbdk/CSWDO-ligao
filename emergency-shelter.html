Required Documents for Assistance

Emergency Shelter Assistance

  • 1 original Incident Certification or Blotter Report from the concerned agency (e.g., Bureau of Fire Protection in case of fire, or police report in case of other incidents)
  • 1 original Certificate of Indigency from the Barangay stating:
    • The name of the applicant
    • Relationship to the household/family
    • Purpose: “Emergency Shelter Assistance”
    • Current crisis condition (e.g., fire, demolition, natural disaster)
  • 1 back-to-back photocopy of a valid government-issued ID (e.g., Voter’s ID, Driver’s License)
  • If no valid ID is available: Certificate of Residency from the Barangay
  • Photo documentation of the damaged house (printed or digital copy)

Application Process

  1. Gather all required documents, especially the official report of the incident and photo evidence of housing damage.
  2. Submit the documents to the CSWDO for review and eligibility screening by the Social Welfare Assistant.
  3. Proceed to the assigned Social Worker for interview, documentation, and preparation of a Certificate of Eligibility.
  4. Submit the application for endorsement to the Office of the City Mayor (Local Chief Executive).
  5. Wait for processing by the City Accounting Office and CSWD administrative staff.
  6. Claim the Emergency Shelter Assistance at the City Treasurer’s Office once ready.
  7. Acknowledge receipt by signing the official logbook.
