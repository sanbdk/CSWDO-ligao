Required Documents for Assistance

Medical Assistance

  • For Hospital Bill
    • Photocopy of recent medical abstract with name, signature, and license number of attending physician
    • Photocopy of final bill duly signed by the billing clerk
    • If already discharged, photocopy of certificate of outstanding balance and promissory note
  • For Medicines/Laboratory Requests
    • Photocopy of recent laboratory test request
    • Photocopy of prescribed medicine/assistive devices with name, signature, and license number of attending physician
    • Original Certificate of Indigency
    • 1 back-to-back photocopy of valid government-issued ID
    • If no ID is available, 1 certificate of residency from barangay
  • For Anti-Rabies Vaccine
    • Original Certificate of Indigency
    • 1 back-to-back photocopy of valid government-issued ID
    • If no ID is available, certificate of residency
    • Photocopy of patient's bite card/schedule of vaccine
    • Barangay blotter
    • Original referral from the City Health Office
    • If ERIG vaccine, price quotation of the ERIG cost
  • Additional Requirements
    • Authorization letter by the patient or nearest kin if someone else will process the assistance
    • Barangay blotter
    • Social case study report from LSWDO if needed

Application Process

  1. Prepare the complete set of required documents.
  2. Submit the documents to the Social Welfare Assistant for verification.
  3. Proceed to the Social Worker for interview and Certificate of Eligibility.
  4. Documents are reviewed and forwarded to the Office of the Local Chief Executive for approval.
  5. Await processing at the City Accounting Office.
