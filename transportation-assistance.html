Required Documents for Assistance

Transportation Assistance

  • 1 original Certificate from the Barangay stating the need for transportation or a Police Blotter (depending on the reason, e.g., stranded, pickpocket incident, etc.)
  • 1 original Certificate of Indigency from the Barangay, indicating:
    • The name of the requesting person
    • Relationship to the beneficiary
    • Purpose as “Transportation Assistance”
  • 1 back-to-back photocopy of a valid government-issued ID (e.g., Voter’s ID, Driver’s License, etc.)
  • If no valid ID is available, a Certificate of Residency issued by the Barangay
  • If the claimant is not a resident of Ligao City, 1 original Police Blotter from the area where the incident occurred

Application Process

  1. Gather all required documents, ensuring the certificate or blotter clearly states the nature of the need (e.g., stranded, urgent travel for medical or legal reason).
  2. Submit the documents to the City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO) for assessment and cross-checking.
  3. Proceed to the assigned Social Worker for interview and issuance of Certificate of Eligibility (if applicable).
  4. Documents are endorsed to the Office of the City Mayor for final approval.
  5. Assistance is processed by the City Accounting Office in coordination with the CSWD.
  6. Receive the Transportation Assistance from the City Treasurer’s Office.
  7. Sign the official logbook to confirm receipt of assistance.
