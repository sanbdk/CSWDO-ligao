Required Documents for Assistance

Senior Citizen ID and Booklet Issuance

  • Duly accomplished Senior Citizen ID Application Form (available at CSWDO or Office of the Senior Citizens Affairs – OSCA)
  • Birth Certificate (or any valid proof of age such as Voter’s ID, Baptismal Certificate, Passport, etc.)
  • Barangay Certificate of Residency
  • 2 pcs. 1x1 ID pictures
  • If for replacement: Affidavit of Loss (in case of lost ID/booklet)

Application Process

  1. Obtain the Senior Citizen Application Form (Available for download).
  2. Fill out the form completely and attach the required documents. Ensure that all entries are accurate and legible.
  3. Submit the documents to the CSWDO or OSCA officer-of-the-day for initial review.
  4. Proceed to the assigned focal person for evaluation and verification. An interview may be conducted if needed.
  5. Wait for the processing of the ID and booklets, which typically takes 3–5 working days.
  6. Claim the ID and booklets at the releasing desk of CSWDO or OSCA. Sign the official logbook as proof of receipt.
  7. Use the ID and booklet to avail of discounts, privileges, and benefits provided under the Expanded Senior Citizens Act of 2010 (RA 9994).
