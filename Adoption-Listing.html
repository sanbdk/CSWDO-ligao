CSWDO Ligao

Providing social welfare services to the citizens of Ligao City.

Vision

The CSWDO envisions equal access to social development assistance and opportunities for all clients and sectors, instilling community participation in a just and gender-fair society free from abuse, hunger, and poverty.

Mission

To effectively and efficiently carry out policies, programs, and services for and with the poor, marginalized, and vulnerable.
