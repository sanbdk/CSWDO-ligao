Required Documents for Burial Assistance

  • 1 photocopy of the Death Certificate (issued by hospital or Local Civil Registry)
  • 1 original Certificate of Indigency from the Barangay indicating the requesting person and relationship to the deceased, with “Burial Assistance” as the stated purpose
  • 1 back-to-back photocopy of a valid government-issued ID (e.g., driver’s license, voter’s ID, TIN ID)
  • If no valid ID is available, Certificate of Residency from the Barangay
  • 1 photocopy of the funeral contract (from the funeral service provider)
  • 1 photocopy of the Permit to Transfer Cadaver (if applicable and required)

Application Process

  1. Gather the required documents, ensuring the indigency certificate and funeral contract clearly mention the burial request.
  2. Submit the complete documents to the Social Welfare Assistant or Aide at the City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO).
  3. Proceed to the Social Worker for an interview and the issuance of the Certificate of Eligibility.
  4. Documents are endorsed to the Office of the Local Chief Executive for review and approval.
  5. Assistance is processed through the City Accounting Office and City Treasurer’s Office.
  6. Claim the burial assistance in cash or cheque at the City Treasurer’s Office.
  7. Sign the official logbook to acknowledge receipt.
